Duner Kebab

This is a Duner Kebab

It is a popular fast food dish, made of meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie.
The meat is then put into a tortilla and the tortilla is filled with diffrent sauces and vegetables

Ingredients

  • tortilla
  • meat
  • potato fries
  • sauces
  • vegetables

Steps:

  1. Cut cooked meat off of the rotisserie
  2. Fill tortilla with vegetables, meat, fries and sauces
  3. Wrap tortilla
